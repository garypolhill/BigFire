Big Fire!

Welcome to Big Fire, a conceptual model of the spread of wildfire for the 360-degree immersive space at The Hutton Hub. The model is conceptual because it simulates the principles of wildfire spread (hot places can ignite nearby areas that have combustible material that is not too damp) without representing the true dynamics of fire in a particular place with specific soil type and vegetation mix.

You can choose between two versions of the video:

  1. 'Muirburn': The fire is started by igniting a strip
  2. 'Barbecue' (or campfire): The fire is started by igniting a point
